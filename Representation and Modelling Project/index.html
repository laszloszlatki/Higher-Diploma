Fishing Quote of the week

"My biggest worry is that my wife (when I'm dead) will sell my fishing gear for what I said I paid for it." ~Koos Brandt

This website is a handy tool for any angler in the Limerick and Clare area but it will also help in any other parts of the country as bait and methods is much the same. We hope to give you the knowledge to have a red letter day and be able to tell a few fisherman tales about "The one that got away". This website will cover 2 different types of fishing. It will show the methods and baits required to catch specific species and locations where you will have the best chance of catching.
